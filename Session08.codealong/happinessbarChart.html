New stuff learnt Errors faced and how I addressed them
Looking at the raw numbers itself did not reveal how life could have been like for the average person in those countries for the specified time period. Looking at the growth rate however, provided insights on this. There were missing data points for some countries which skewed the actual growth rate of happiness. I resolved this by carefully removing those outliers from the growth rate calculations. Otherwise, I would end up with an inaccurate data visualization.
